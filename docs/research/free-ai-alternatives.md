## ⚠️ HONEST CAVEATS

1. **Rate limits are real** — With 1,500 RPD on Gemini, you can handle ~100 users/day making 15 requests each. For more, use multiple providers.
2. **"Free" means data may be used for training** — Google/Mistral may use your prompts to improve models. Don't send sensitive mining data.
3. **Google Earth Engine** — Free but requires approval (24-48hrs). Non-commercial use only unless you get commercial access.
4. **Supabase free tier** — Database pauses after 1 week of inactivity (auto-resumes on request).
5. **Model quality tradeoff** — Free models are good but not GPT-4-level for everything. Test thoroughly.
6. **Multi-provider strategy is essential** — No single free provider covers all needs. Use Gemini + Mistral + Groq together.
